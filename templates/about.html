About This Starter Kit

This starter kit is a lightweight foundation for Flask projects built from patterns I’ve reused across real production systems — mainly web platforms and SaaS tools in automotive marketing, GPS, and analytics.

The goal is not to be a full framework or a bloated boilerplate. This is designed to stay small, clear, and production-minded while still covering the essential setup most projects need.

Goals

  • Keep structure clean and understandable.
  • Provide an SEO-friendly layout from day one.
  • Stay flexible without over-engineering.

Roadmap

Over time I plan to expand this kit with example pages for things like authentication, dashboards, and contact workflows — but always keeping the foundation lean.

GitHub: github.com/sebfloapps/flask-seo-bootstrap-starter-kit
